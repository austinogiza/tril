About Trillium Stars

Trillium Stars British Int’l School was born in September 2013, and has cultivated and invested on the idea of bringing out the best of our pupils while they are still in their early years, and to elevate the personal, emotional, intellectual and academic, proficiency of our precious children.

Trillium Stars British Int’l School provides individual attention to challenges facing our children and supports each child to enable him or her perform to the best of their ability, with the support from competent, well trained and skilled teachers

Learn more

Our Philosophy

Trillium Stars British Int’l School provides complete learning, recognising that each child has a different learning speed, aiming at making the best use of the potential of all our pupils in terms of their intellectual, cultural, moral and spiritual development.

Our Mission Statement

Trillium Stars British Int’l School recognises that every cbhild is an individual, that all children need to succeed therefore. Trillium Stars British Int’l School respects the individual needs of children, fosters a caring and creative environment and emphasizes the social, emotional, physical, intellectual development of each child

Our Partner With Parents

We recognise that our play a huge part in the success of their children education today. and so we maintain formal contacts with parents always, through modest phone calls, periodic newsletter and questionaires and parent teacher forum.
