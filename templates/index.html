About Trillium Stars

Trillium Stars British Int’l School was born in September 2013, and has cultivated and invested on the idea of bringing out the best of our pupils while they are still in their early years, and to elevate the personal, emotional, intellectual and academic, proficiency of our precious children.

Trillium Stars British Int’l School provides individual attention to challenges facing our children and supports each child to enable him or her perform to the best of their ability, with the support from competent, well trained and skilled teachers
